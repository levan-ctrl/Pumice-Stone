🎯 Strategic Goal: 4-Month Stock Buffer

A 4-month buffer means holding 14,000 Terracotta + 1,400 Gray + 1,400 Black units on hand at all times (4 months × 3,500 / 350 units). This eliminates any stockout risk and gives you leverage to negotiate bigger discounts on reorders.
